Saag

Saag is a traditional Punjabi dish made from mustard greens and other leafy vegetables such as spinach. The greens are cooked slowly with spices like garlic, ginger, turmeric, and red chili powder, creating a rich, velvety dish. Saag has a slightly bitter flavor from the mustard greens, which is balanced by the spices. It is usually served with makki di roti (cornmeal flatbread) and a dollop of butter or ghee. This dish is particularly popular during the winter months in northern Punjab, as the warming spices help to combat the chill of the season.
