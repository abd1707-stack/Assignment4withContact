Mutton Yakhni

Mutton Yakhni is a flavorful broth made from simmering mutton with a mixture of spices, including cumin, coriander, cinnamon, and cloves. The meat is cooked until it’s tender and the flavors of the spices have infused into the broth. The dish is light yet aromatic, with the tender meat and flavorful broth served with plain rice. Yakhni is often consumed as a comfort food, particularly during the winter months, as its warmth and richness are both soothing and nourishing.
