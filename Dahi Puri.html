Dahi Puri

Dahi Puri is a type of street food that features small, hollow, crispy puris filled with a mixture of chickpeas, potatoes, yogurt, and tangy chutneys. The puris are typically stuffed with a combination of spiced boiled potatoes, chickpeas, and yogurt, then drizzled with tamarind and mint chutneys. The dish is garnished with finely chopped coriander, pomegranate seeds, and sev (crispy chickpea flour noodles). Dahi Puri is a refreshing, tangy, and crunchy treat, ideal for hot summer days, and is often sold by street vendors in bustling markets and food courts.
