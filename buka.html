Bun Kebab

Bun Kebab is a type of Pakistani burger, consisting of a spiced beef, chicken, or mutton patty placed inside a soft bun. The patty is typically seasoned with onions, green chilies, coriander, and a blend of spices. The burger is often garnished with lettuce, tomatoes, onions, and tangy chutneys, providing a balance of spicy and fresh flavors. This fast-food favorite is commonly eaten as a snack or a light meal, especially in urban areas of Pakistan, where it's available from roadside vendors and restaurants.
