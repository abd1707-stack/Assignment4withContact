Kheer

Kheer is a beloved Pakistani dessert made from rice, milk, sugar, and cardamom. The rice is cooked in milk until it absorbs the liquid and becomes tender. The dish is sweetened with sugar and flavored with cardamom, and garnished with chopped nuts, such as almonds and pistachios, as well as dried fruits like raisins. Kheer is often served during festive occasions such as Eid, weddings, and family celebrations, and is typically enjoyed chilled or at room temperature.
