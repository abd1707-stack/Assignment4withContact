Chicken Shawarma

Shawarma is a Middle Eastern-inspired dish that has become extremely popular in Pakistan, particularly as street food. The chicken is marinated with a mixture of yogurt, garlic, lemon juice, and a variety of spices like cumin, coriander, and paprika. The marinated chicken is then cooked on a vertical rotisserie until it’s perfectly tender and slightly crispy. Shawarma is typically served wrapped in pita or flatbread with fresh vegetables such as tomatoes, cucumbers, and lettuce, and drizzled with garlic sauce or tahini.
