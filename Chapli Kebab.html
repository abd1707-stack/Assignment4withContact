Chapli Kebab

Chapli Kebab is a traditional Pashtun dish that consists of ground meat, usually beef or mutton, mixed with spices, herbs, onions, tomatoes, and green chilies. This mixture is shaped into flat, round patties and shallow-fried in ghee or oil until golden and crispy. The spices used include coriander seeds, cumin, ginger, garlic, and garam masala, giving the kebabs a fragrant and spicy flavor. These kebabs are often served with naan or paratha and a side of yogurt or chutney. The crispy exterior and juicy interior make chapli kebabs a favorite among meat lovers in Pakistan.
