Seekh Kebabs

Seekh Kebabs are minced meat kebabs, typically made from beef, mutton, or chicken, mixed with a variety of spices, onions, green chilies, and herbs. The meat mixture is formed onto skewers and grilled or baked, often in a tandoor oven. The spices used in seekh kebabs include cumin, coriander, garam masala, and chili powder, giving them a smoky, spicy flavor. Seekh kebabs are a favorite street food in Pakistan and are often served with naan, raita, and fresh salad, making them a popular appetizer or snack.
