Zarda

Zarda is a sweet rice dish that is prepared by cooking basmati rice with sugar, milk, cardamom, saffron, and a variety of dried fruits such as raisins, almonds, and pistachios. The rice is first boiled, then combined with the sweetened milk mixture and cooked until it absorbs the flavors. Zarda is often served on special occasions such as weddings, Eid, and family gatherings. The addition of saffron and cardamom gives the dish its characteristic aroma and color, making it a visually and sensorially delightful dessert.
