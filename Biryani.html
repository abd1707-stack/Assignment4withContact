Biryani

Biryani is one of Pakistanâ€™s most iconic dishes, featuring layers of fragrant basmati rice and marinated meat (chicken, mutton, or beef) cooked with a blend of aromatic spices like cumin, cardamom, cinnamon, and cloves. The meat is marinated in yogurt and a mixture of spices that tenderize and flavor it, while the rice absorbs the essence of the meat and spices as they cook together. A garnish of fried onions, fresh cilantro, and a boiled egg on top adds flavor and texture. Often served with cooling yogurt-based raita or a tangy salad, Biryani can be found throughout Pakistan, with each region having its own variations, such as Karachi biryani, which tends to be spicier.
