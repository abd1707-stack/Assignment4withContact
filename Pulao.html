Pulao

Pulao is a mild, fragrant rice dish cooked with meat (usually chicken, mutton, or beef) and a variety of spices. Unlike Biryani, which is more complex and layered, pulao is a simpler dish where the rice is cooked in a broth made from the meat, along with spices like cinnamon, cloves, cumin, and cardamom. Sometimes, vegetables such as peas, carrots, or beans are added to the dish for extra flavor and nutrition. Pulao is often served as a side dish with curries, or on its own with a side of raita or chutney.
