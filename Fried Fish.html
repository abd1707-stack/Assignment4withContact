Fried fish

Fried fish is a popular dish, especially in coastal areas of Pakistan, such as Karachi and Gwadar. The fish is marinated with a mixture of spices, including red chili powder, turmeric, ginger, garlic, and lemon juice, and then fried until crispy. Common fish varieties used in this dish include Rohu, Surmai, and Pomfret. The fish is served hot, often with naan or rice, and a side of salad or raita. This dish is a favorite during Ramadan, especially for Iftar, when the crispiness of the fish is complemented by refreshing drinks.
