Keema

Keema refers to minced meat, usually beef, mutton, or chicken, cooked with onions, tomatoes, green chilies, and a blend of spices like turmeric, cumin, coriander, and garam masala. The minced meat is sautéed until browned, and then cooked with the aromatic spices until it absorbs the flavors. Keema is often served with naan, paratha, or rice and is enjoyed for breakfast, lunch, or dinner. It’s a versatile dish that can be made with various additions, including peas, potatoes, or boiled eggs, to add texture and flavor.
